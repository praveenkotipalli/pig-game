How to Play 🎮🕹️

->First player who reaches the score >=100 is the Winner.
->If "1" appers on the dice , you will lose all your current score and the playing chance will go to the next player.
->Use hold button if you want to add your current score to the main score and to give the chance to other player to play, so you can avoid the risk of getting "1" on the next roll(*because if you get "1" you will lose your current score i.e,current score you got on this trail will not be added to your main score)
